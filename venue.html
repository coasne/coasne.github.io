2023 French/German Adsorption Conference

Venue


The conference will take place in the site of ECPM (European School of Chemistry, Polymer and Materials) located on the Cronenbourg campus, 10 minutes by bus (bus G -- direction Espace Européen de l’Entreprise -- Stop “ARAGO”) from Strasbourg train station.
